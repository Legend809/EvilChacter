Rotten-inator

Dr. Doofenshmirtz has plans to enter his family meatloaf recipe into the competition using "hate" as the special ingredient (as opposed to love). And just in case, he got the Rotten-inator to rot up his other opponents' meatloaves.

Rain-inator

He uses it to make it rain on the Tri-State Area so that he could watch El Matador De Amor without soccer game interruptions.

Combine-inator

He plans to combine an island and Capital of the Tri-State Area so legally he would be the ruler of the Tri-State Area.

Taffy inator

Dr. Doofenshmirtz invents the Salt Water Taffy-inator to give cavities to every kid in the Tri State Area.

Dodo-Bird-Incubator-inator

Dr. Doofenshmirtz makes an egg turn into a dodo bird egg and is convinced that it is a fierce bird-monster that can help him take over the Tri-state area, but discovers the exact opposite when the egg hatches.

Stain-inator

Dr. Doofenshmirtz plans on ruining his brother's art unveiling by using his Stain-inator to ruin a mysterious painting, but as it turns out, the painting is Dr. Doofenshmirtz's very own masterpiece that he created when he was a youth back in Gimmelshtump.
